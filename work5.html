005 : Photography

I love taking photos, discovering new places, and watching the sunset. Especially I moved to Providence Rhode Island from Turkey, I found photography as a way to immortalize what I enjoy the most - the nature, sunsets, and my family and friends.

"Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything."

Aaron Siskind
